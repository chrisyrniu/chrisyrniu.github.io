Hi! My name is Yaru (Chris) Niu. I am currently pursing my Masters in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech. I am working with [Rohan Paleja](https://rohanpaleja.com/) at CORE Lab under [Prof. Matthew Gombolay](https://core-robotics.gatech.edu/people/matthew-gombolay/). My research interests include human-robot interaction, reinforcement learning and multi-agent coordination.
 
 Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from [South China University of Technology](https://www.scut.edu.cn/en/), where I conducted research projects with [Mr. Lingdong Kong](http://ldkong.com/) in [Bionic Intelligent Robot (BIR) Lab](https://bir-lab.github.io/), supervised by Prof. Zhijun Zhang. In the fall of 2018, I was a visiting student at UC Berkeley. During the summer of  2018, I worked with [Nafiul Rashid](https://sites.uci.edu/nafiulrashid/) at [AICPS Lab](https://aicps.eng.uci.edu/) in UC Irvine, supervised by Prof. Al Faruque. I was involved in the Health IoT project and worked on machine learning for classification of time-series data.
 
In my spare time, I enjoy playing basketball and watching movies.
